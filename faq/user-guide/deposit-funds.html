3. DEPOSIT FUNDS

Wowoo Exchange Singapore partners with Xfers, a leading payment gateway licensed by the Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS). With Xfers, you can transfer funds from your Singapore bank account into the Wowoo Exchange Singapore platform.

A

Login to your Wowoo Exchange account and navigate to Wallets.

B

  • Select ‘Deposit’ next to SGD (see below).
  • Click on Connect to Xfers.
  • Follow the instructions to create a Xfers account.
  • You will be prompted to enter your mobile number.
  • Verify your identity on Xfers by uploading a copy of your. passport and proof of address.

C

  • Upon approval from Xfers, you will be able to top up SGD to buy cryptocurrency.
  • Enter the amount (you will be prompted for a Google Authenticator code).

D

Click on Deposit from Xfers to top up your Wowoo SGD wallet.
