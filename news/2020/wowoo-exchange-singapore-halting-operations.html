Wowoo Exchange Singapore is halting operations

The management of Wowoo Exchange Singapore (WES) is sad to announce that due to strategic realignment, WES will be halting operations in Singapore with immediate effect.

All users can be assured that all assets in their WES accounts are safe and being held securely. Users are asked to withdraw all their assets from the WES platform by 30-Mar-20 at the latest. All Wowoo Exchange fees will be waived until this date.

There will be further announcements regarding the scope and timing of the closure in due course.
