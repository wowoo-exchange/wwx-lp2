Without processing your Personal Data for such purposes, we cannot respond to your requests and ensure your uninterrupted use of the Services.

Without processing your Personal Data, we may not be able to ensure the security of Wowoo Services.

To further improve our Services

We process your Personal Data to better understand the way you use and interact with Wowoo Services. In addition, we use such information to customise, measure, and improve Wowoo Services and the content and layout of the Website and other Wowoo Materials, and to develop new services.
